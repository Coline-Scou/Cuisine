## Ingredients (serves about 4)

- **6 apples** (Golden or Granny Smith type)
- **1/2 cup** water (about 125 ml)
- **1 tablespoon** lemon juice
- **2 tablespoons** sugar (optional, to taste)
- **1/2 teaspoon** cinnamon (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Preparing the apples**:
   - Peel the apples, remove the core and cut into small pieces.
   - Place the apple pieces in a large saucepan.


2. **Cooking**:
   - Add the water and lemon juice to the pan with the apples.
   - Bring to the boil over medium heat, then reduce to low.
   - Laisser mijoter pendant **15 √† 20 minutes**, en remuant de temps en temps, jusqu'√† ce que les pommes soient tendres.


3. **Blend the compote**:
   - Once the apples are cooked, remove from the heat.
   - Use a blender or potato masher to obtain a smooth compote. For a chunky compote, crush lightly with a wooden spoon.
   - Add sugar and cinnamon to taste, then stir.


4. **Tasting**:
   - Serve the compote hot or cold, as you prefer.
   - **Good to know**: The compote can be stored in the refrigerator for 3 to 4 days.
